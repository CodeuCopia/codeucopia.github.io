About Us

pAIthon is a club at Walter Payton College Prep dedicated to bringing accessible and interactive computer science lessons to young minds. In many schools around Chicago, computer science classes are not priortized, and students lack a platform to explore this multi-faceted. Having been there ourselves, we wanted to create a space where students could engage with coding and technology in a fun and supportive environment.

Our mission is to empower all students with the tools and knowledge to thrive in the tech world, sparking curiosity and inspiring the next generation of innovators.
