About Us

CodeuCopia is a club at Walter Payton College Prep dedicated to bringing accessible and interactive computer science lessons to young minds. In many schools around Chicago, computer science classes are not prioritized, and students lack a platform to explore this multi-faceted subject. Having been there ourselves, we wanted to create a space where students could engage with coding and technology in a fun and supportive environment.
